# Why Adopt This

## Before

Without a tool like this, saved X bookmarks often remain:

- trapped inside the platform UI
- hard to reuse outside one account/session
- awkward to inspect with AI agents
- hard to refresh into a stable local artifact
- difficult to turn into a reusable knowledge asset

---

## After

With this approach, the same bookmark corpus becomes:

- a local HTML view for humans
- a structured JSON bundle for AI agents
- a schedulable refresh target
- a portable artifact you can inspect, archive, and mirror

---

## Main benefits

### 1. One corpus, two modes of reuse
You do not have to choose between a human-facing gallery and machine-readable data.

### 2. Local-first trust
The outputs are inspectable files, not opaque cloud state.

### 3. Better long-term reuse
Saved posts become a reusable knowledge pack instead of a passive bookmark pile.

### 4. Easier automation
Once the upstream export exists, refresh can be pointed at one stable command.

### 5. Safer public sharing boundary
The sanitize layer helps separate private operational data from public-safe bundle artifacts.
